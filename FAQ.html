Frequently Asked Questions

Question 1: What makes you salads different?
Answer: We focus on using high-quality, fresh ingredients that make our salads burst with flavour. Every salad is designed to be healthy, tasty, and satisfying-not just light food, but rather full meals to enjoy.

Question 2: Are your ingredients fresh?
Answer: Yes. Utilizing fresh ingredients is a must for our business. Not only are the ingredients fresh, but our salads are made as close as possible to the agreed collection time, maximising freshness and ensuring each bowl tastes as good as it looks.

Question 3: Do your salads include protein?
Answer: Absolutely. We offer a large range of salad variations, many of which include chicken and other additions to help fuel you for the day.
